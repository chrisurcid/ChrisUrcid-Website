Diiffusors diffuse any light, like sunlight or natural light. The use of batteries and plugs is essential when you are on set. One of my top skills is being capable of having on-set experience and using it to help others in the workforce. I am very well with others when it comes to group projects. I have experience with lighting techniques and knowing when a shot needs to be lit up and dimmed down. With camera experience, I can explore the many angles that a shot can have.
